To You!

When we first met through this arranged marriage, I never thought my heart would feel this way. At first, we were just two strangers brought together by destiny… but slowly, I realized how much I look forward to your smile, your words, and your presence. You might not know this, but I already like you — heaven knows, earth knows, everyone knows… maybe except you. When you’re happy, your joy becomes mine, and when you’re quiet or sad, I just want to hold your hand and be your comfort I want to walk this journey with you, not as two people tied by tradition, but as two souls bound by love. ❤️
